<p><p>The blood-staunching sponges market is segmented into three main types: absorbable gelatin, chitosan, and collagen sponges. Absorbable gelatin sponges are derived from animal collagen and provide rapid hemostatic properties, ideal for minor surgical procedures. Chitosan sponges, derived from crustacean shells, possess antimicrobial properties and are effective in controlling bleeding. Collagen sponges, sourced from bovine or porcine tissues, promote wound healing while enhancing clot formation. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to various surgical needs and preferences in medical practices.</p></p>
