Acid Chloride-based resins utilize acid chlorides to enhance thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. On the other hand, Diisocyanate-based resins involve the reaction of diisocyanates with polyamines, resulting in flexible, durable products ideal for coatings and adhesives. Both types aim to provide environmentally safer alternatives by eliminating harmful solvent NMP while maintaining performance characteristics.</p></p>
