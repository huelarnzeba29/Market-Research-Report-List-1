<p><strong>The MRI Tomography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Field</li><li>Midfield</li><li>High Field</li><li>Ultra-High Field</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRI tomography system market is categorized into four main types based on magnetic field strength. Low field MRI systems typically operate below 1.0 Tesla, providing basic imaging for specific applications. Midfield systems, ranging from 1.0 to 2.0 Tesla, offer improved image quality and versatility. High field MRI, operating between 3.0 to 7.0 Tesla, delivers superior resolution and is commonly used in advanced diagnostics. Ultra-high field MRI systems, exceeding 7.0 Tesla, are utilized for cutting-edge research and highly detailed imaging.</p></p>
