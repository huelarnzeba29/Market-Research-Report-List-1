<p><strong>The Kraft Lignin Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Softwood Kraft Lignin</li><li>Hardwood Kraft Lignin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Kraft lignin products market is primarily segmented into two categories: softwood Kraft lignin and hardwood Kraft lignin. Softwood Kraft lignin is derived from coniferous trees, typically offering higher molecular weight and better solubility, making it suitable for applications in bioplastics and adhesives. Hardwood Kraft lignin comes from deciduous trees, generally possessing lower molecular weight with diverse functional groups, which enhance its use in agricultural chemicals and as a filler in various industrial products. Both types have significant industrial applications and growing market demand.</p></p>
