<p><p>The anemometer market can be segmented into several types, including cup anemometers, vane anemometers, hot-wire anemometers, and others. Cup anemometers measure wind speed using rotating cups, ideal for meteorological applications. Vane anemometers utilize a rotating blade aligned with the wind direction, suited for HVAC systems. Hot-wire anemometers consist of a heated wire that detects airflow changes, effective for precise measurements. Other types encompass advanced and specialized instruments, catering to various industries and research needs.</p></p>
