<p><strong>The Carbon-Carbon Composite Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Vapor Deposition</li><li>Liquid Impregnation Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon-Carbon Composite Material market features primarily two production methods: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process (LIP). CVD involves depositing carbon from gaseous precursors onto a substrate, creating a dense and high-performance composite ideal for high-temperature applications. In contrast, LIP involves saturating a fibrous matrix with liquid resin or pitch, followed by carbonization under heat, resulting in a strong and lightweight material. Both methods cater to sectors such as aerospace, automotive, and defense, capitalizing on their superior mechanical properties.</p></p>
