Corrugated sheets are characterized by their wave-like structure, offering enhanced strength and impact resistance, commonly used in industrial applications. Solid sheets offer high clarity and strength, suitable for applications like safety glazing and signs, combining versatility with aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
