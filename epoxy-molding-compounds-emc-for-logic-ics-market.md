<p><p>Epoxy Molding Compounds (EMC) are critical materials used for packaging logic integrated circuits (ICs). The market is categorized into two types: Solid EMC and Liquid EMC. Solid EMC is a pre-mixed, solid form that is typically melted and then molded around the ICs, offering excellent thermal stability and durability. Liquid EMC, on the other hand, is in a viscous state that flows easily during the molding process, providing better encapsulation and fill characteristics. Both types serve distinct applications, enhancing device performance and reliability.</p></p>
