<p><strong>The Resin Dental Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Macrofilled Filler</li><li>Microfilled Filler</li><li>Hybrid Filler</li><li>Nanofilled Filler</li><li>Bulk Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin dental material market includes various types of fillers that enhance the performance and aesthetics of dental composites. Macrofilled fillers are larger particles, providing strength but less polishability. Microfilled fillers are smaller, offering excellent polishability and aesthetic results but lower strength. Hybrid fillers combine both for balanced properties, while nanofilled fillers utilize nanoparticles to achieve superior strength and polishability. Bulk fillers allow for easier application in larger restorations, decreasing chair time while maintaining good mechanical properties.</p></p>
