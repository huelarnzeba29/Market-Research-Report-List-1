<p><strong>The Industrial Inkjet Printheads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Printheads</li><li>Thermal Printheads</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial inkjet printheads market primarily consists of three types: piezoelectric printheads, thermal printheads, and others. Piezoelectric printheads utilize electric charges to control the ink ejection process, enabling precise droplet formation, making them suitable for a range of inks. Thermal printheads generate heat to vaporize ink, creating bubbles that eject droplets, commonly used in high-speed applications. The "others" category includes emerging technologies and specialized printheads that serve niche industries and specific printing requirements, broadening application horizons.</p></p>
