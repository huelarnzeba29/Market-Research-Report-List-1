<p><p>The DC Signal Relays market is categorized into two main types based on mounting methods: TH (Through Hole) Mounting and SMT (Surface Mount Technology) Mounting. TH mounting is characterized by relays that are inserted through holes in a printed circuit board, offering strong mechanical support and easy soldering. Conversely, SMT mounting involves components directly affixed to the surface of the PCB, enabling higher density and efficiency. Both types fulfill specific application requirements, influencing design choices in electronic systems.</p></p>
