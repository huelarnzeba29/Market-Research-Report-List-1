<p><strong>The Cheese Coagulation Detection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cheese coagulation detection systems are categorized into contact and non-contact markets based on their operational methods. Contact systems involve direct interaction with the milk or curd, using physical probes to measure properties like pH or viscosity. In contrast, non-contact systems utilize advanced technologies such as infrared or ultrasound sensors to analyze coagulation without touching the material. Both types aim to enhance cheese production efficiency by providing real-time data on coagulation stages, ensuring consistent quality and optimal processing times.</p></p>
