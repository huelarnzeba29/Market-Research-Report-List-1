<p><p>Continuous vacuum packaging machines come in several types: Mechanical extrusion machines utilize a piston mechanism for sealing, offering reliability and efficiency. Extracting vacuum machines operate by removing air from packaging, ensuring longer shelf life for products. Chamber vacuum machines seal items within a chamber, providing consistent vacuum levels and are ideal for bulk packaging. Thermoforming machines heat plastic sheets, forming them around products before vacuum sealing, perfect for custom packaging solutions. Each type caters to different packaging needs across various industries.</p></p>
