<p><p>Helical bevel geared motors, designed for efficiency and torque transmission, are categorized into two primary types based on shaft design: hollow shaft and solid shaft. Hollow shaft geared motors feature a cylindrical opening, allowing for the easy integration of shafts or other components, making them ideal for compact applications. In contrast, solid shaft geared motors provide enhanced durability and torque handling capabilities, suited for heavier loads and demanding environments. Both types serve distinct applications across various industries, optimizing performance and space.</p></p>
