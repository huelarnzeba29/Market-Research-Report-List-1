<p><p>The home use water purifier market is primarily categorized into three types: RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers, UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers, and other systems. RO purifiers use a membrane to remove impurities, making them ideal for areas with high TDS (Total Dissolved Solids). UV purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses, ensuring microbiological safety. Other purifiers may include gravity-based systems or activated carbon filters, addressing specific purification needs based on water quality and user preferences.</p></p>
