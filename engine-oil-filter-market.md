<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine oil filter market is categorized into three main types: mechanical, rotary, and others. Mechanical filters utilize a medium to physically trap and remove contaminants from the oil, ensuring engine cleanliness. Rotary filters operate using centrifugal force to separate contaminants from the oil, enhancing filtration efficiency. The "other" category encompasses specialized or hybrid systems, such as magnetic or electrostatic filters, which employ alternative technologies for enhanced filtration. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining engine performance and longevity.</p></p>
