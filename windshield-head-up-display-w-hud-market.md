<p><p>The Windshield Head-up Display (W-HUD) market comprises two main types: front mounting and rear mounting. Front mounting displays project vital information directly onto the windshield, enhancing driver visibility while minimizing distraction. These are typically used in advanced driver-assistance systems and luxury vehicles. In contrast, rear mounting systems are positioned behind the windshield, projecting information that can be less intrusive yet equally informative. Both types aim to improve safety and navigation by allowing drivers to maintain their focus on the road.</p></p>
