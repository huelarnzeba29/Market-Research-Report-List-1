<p><p>The water-free urinals market includes various technologies designed to reduce water usage in sanitation systems. Liquid sealant cartridges utilize a special liquid to create a seal, preventing odors while allowing waste to pass through. Membrane traps use a non-return valve to block odors while enabling waste disposal. Biological blocks contain enzymes that break down waste and neutralize odors, promoting sanitation. Mechanical balls function as a physical barrier that separates waste from the air, effectively controlling odors without water.</p></p>
