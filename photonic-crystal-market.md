<p><p>The photonic crystal market is categorized into three types based on dimensionality. One-Dimensional Photonic Crystals are structured with layers of alternating materials, manipulating light in a single direction, ideal for applications like waveguides. Two-Dimensional Photonic Crystals involve periodic structures in two dimensions, enhancing light control for devices such as sensors and optical switches. Three-Dimensional Photonic Crystals feature complex architectures that enable full control over light propagation, making them suitable for advanced applications in telecommunications and optical computing.</p></p>
