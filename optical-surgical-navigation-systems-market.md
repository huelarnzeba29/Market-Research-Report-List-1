<p><ul><li>Hybrid Navigation</li><li>Optical Navigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Surgical Navigation Systems consist of two main types: Hybrid Navigation and Optical Navigation. Hybrid Navigation combines multiple technologies, such as optical tracking and electromagnetic sensors, enhancing accuracy in complex surgical procedures. It offers real-time feedback while adapting to various surgical environments. Conversely, Optical Navigation relies solely on advanced optical tracking systems, utilizing cameras and markers to guide surgeons through procedures. This method offers high precision and minimal interference, making it particularly effective in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
