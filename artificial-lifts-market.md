<p><strong>The Artificial Lifts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump Assisted</li><li>Gas Assisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial lift market comprises two main types: pump-assisted and gas-assisted systems. Pump-assisted methods, primarily utilizing electric submersible pumps (ESPs) and rod pumps, enhance fluid movement from wells, improving production rates in liquid-rich reservoirs. In contrast, gas-assisted lifts, such as gas lift systems, use injected gas to reduce fluid density, facilitating easier extraction of oil and gas. Both methods aim to optimize extraction processes, improve efficiency, and maximize recovery from various subsurface conditions in oil and gas operations.</p></p>
