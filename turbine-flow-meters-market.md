<p><p>Turbine flow meters are precision instruments used to measure the flow rate of liquids and gases. They operate on the principle of fluid dynamics, where the flow of a fluid causes a rotor to turn within the meter, generating a pulse signal proportional to the flow rate. These meters are known for their accuracy, reliability, and ability to handle a wide range of viscosities and flow rates, making them ideal for various applications in industries such as oil and gas, water management, and food processing.</p><p>The Turbine Flow Meters Market is poised for substantial growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period.
