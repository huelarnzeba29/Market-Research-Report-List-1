<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogen generation market includes traditional alkaline electrolysers and proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolysers, each serving distinct applications and advantages. Alkaline electrolysers, the most established technology, utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte for hydrogen production, offering cost-effectiveness and durability. In contrast, PEM electrolysers use a solid polymer electrolyte, enabling higher efficiency and quicker response times, making them ideal for variable renewable energy sources. Both technologies are crucial in advancing clean hydrogen production and supporting a sustainable energy transition.</p></p>
