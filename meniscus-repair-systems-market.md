<p><p>The Meniscus Repair Systems market comprises various techniques for repairing meniscal injuries in the knee. All-Inside Meniscal Repair Systems allow for suturing within the joint, minimizing invasiveness. Outside-In systems involve accessing the meniscus from outside the joint, enabling repair via sutures placed inside. Inside-Out approaches require a combination of internal and external access for suture placement. Meniscal Root Repair Systems specifically target root tears crucial for knee stability, ensuring optimal recovery and joint function. Each approach caters to different injury types and surgical preferences.</p></p>
