<p><p>The Belt Hoists market is primarily divided into two types: Electric Belt Hoists and Manual Belt Hoists. Electric Belt Hoists are powered by electrical energy, enabling efficient and rapid lifting, making them ideal for heavy loads and industrial applications. They feature automatic controls for enhanced safety and precision. Manual Belt Hoists, on the other hand, rely on human effort for operation, offering a more cost-effective solution for lighter loads and applications where electricity isn’t available, providing flexibility and portability.</p></p>
