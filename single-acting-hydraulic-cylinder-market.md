Tie rod cylinders feature external rods for better strength and easy maintenance, making them suitable for general industrial use. Welded cylinders offer a compact design with robust construction, ideal for high-pressure applications. Telescopic cylinders extend more than their length, perfect for limited spaces where long stroke lengths are needed. Mill type cylinders are heavily built for extreme conditions, commonly used in heavy machinery and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
