<p><p>The glass cleaning robot market is primarily divided into two types: drag-wiper and roller-wiper systems. Drag-wiper robots use a flat wiper that moves horizontally across the glass surface, effectively removing dirt and grime. In contrast, roller-wiper robots utilize rotating rollers that grip the glass while cleaning, enabling a more thorough and efficient clean. Both types cater to different surface shapes and cleaning needs, offering users advanced solutions for maintaining cleanliness in high-rise buildings and large glass surfaces.</p></p>
