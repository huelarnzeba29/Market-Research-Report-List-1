<p><p>The gas log sets market is primarily categorized into two types: vented and ventless gas log sets. Vented gas log sets require a chimney or flue for exhaust, closely mimicking traditional wood-burning fireplaces and offering realistic flames and heat. In contrast, ventless gas log sets operate without a vent, allowing for greater installation flexibility and efficiency. While ventless options generate more heat and can be installed in various settings, they may have stricter regulations due to potential indoor air quality concerns.</p></p>
