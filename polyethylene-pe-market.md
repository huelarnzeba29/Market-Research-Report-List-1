<p><p>Polyethylene (PE) is a widely used thermoplastic polymer with various types including High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE offers high strength and rigidity, making it suitable for containers and piping. LDPE is flexible and resistant to chemicals, ideal for film applications and packaging. LLDPE combines the properties of HDPE and LDPE, providing better tensile strength and flexibility, commonly used in stretch films and flexible packaging products. Each type caters to specific market needs.</p></p>
