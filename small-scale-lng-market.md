<p><strong>The Small Scale LNG Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquefaction Terminal</li><li>Regasification Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small-scale LNG market consists of two main types: liquefaction terminals and regasification terminals. Liquefaction terminals focus on converting natural gas into liquid form for storage and transportation, catering to smaller markets or remote locations. In contrast, regasification terminals receive LNG shipments and convert them back to gas for distribution in local energy markets. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the accessibility and flexibility of LNG, facilitating energy supply in regions with limited infrastructure.</p></p>
