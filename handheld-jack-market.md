<p><p>The handheld jack market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and mechanical. Pneumatic jacks utilize compressed air to lift heavy loads, offering quick operation and portability, ideal for automotive applications. Hydraulic jacks use fluid pressure, providing significant lifting power while remaining compact, making them suitable for various industrial tasks. Mechanical jacks rely on a system of gears or levers for lifting, often requiring manual effort, and are commonly used in construction and maintenance jobs for their simplicity and reliability.</p></p>
