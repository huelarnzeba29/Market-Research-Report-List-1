<p><p>Rare Earth Waste Treatment and Recycling encompasses methods for recovering valuable rare earth elements from waste products. Metallurgical recycling involves the reprocessing of discarded materials using high-temperature techniques to separate and purify rare earth metals, ensuring efficient recovery. Extraction recycling, on the other hand, focuses on leaching and solvent extraction methods to selectively dissolve and isolate rare earth elements from waste streams. Both approaches contribute to sustainability by reducing environmental impact and minimizing the need for new rare earth mining.</p></p>
