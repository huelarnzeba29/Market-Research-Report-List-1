<p><ul><li>Articulated Robots</li><li>Parallel Robots</li><li>SCARA Robots</li><li>Cylindrical Robots</li><li>Cartesian Robots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial robotics market comprises several types, each designed for specific tasks. Articulated robots feature rotary joints for versatile movement, ideal for complex assembly and welding. Parallel robots, with their fixed base and moving arms, excel in rapid, precise tasks such as pick-and-place operations. SCARA robots combine rotational movement with vertical motion, perfect for assembly tasks. Cylindrical robots operate within cylindrical coordinate systems, suitable for loading and unloading. Cartesian robots use linear movements along three axes, effective for straightforward tasks like packaging.</p></p>
