<p><ul><li>Cationic Antistatic Agent</li><li>Anionic Antistatic Agent</li><li>Nonionic Antistatic Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The durable antistatic agent market encompasses three primary types: cationic, anionic, and nonionic agents. Cationic antistatic agents carry a positive charge and effectively reduce static electricity in various materials. Anionic antistatic agents possess a negative charge, offering similar static control but with different application properties. Nonionic antistatic agents, which are neutral, provide versatile performance across a wide range of formulations without influencing the material's ionic balance. Each type is essential for enhancing product stability and usability in industries such as textiles, plastics, and electronics.</p></p>
