<p><p>Single-axis solar collectors are categorized into low, medium, and high-temperature collectors based on their thermal output. Low-temperature collectors, typically used for residential applications, harness sunlight for heating water at temperatures up to 80°C. Medium-temperature collectors, suitable for industrial processes, achieve temperatures between 80°C and 400°C, enabling applications like space heating and refrigeration. High-temperature collectors, used in concentrated solar power systems, reach temperatures above 400°C, primarily for electricity generation and advanced thermal applications in industries.</p></p>
