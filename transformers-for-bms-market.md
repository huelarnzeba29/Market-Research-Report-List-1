<p><p>Transformers for Battery Management Systems (BMS) are categorized into Single Channel and Dual Channel markets based on their operational capabilities. Single Channel transformers support monitoring and managing one battery pack, ideal for simpler applications. In contrast, Dual Channel transformers can simultaneously handle two battery packs, enhancing efficiency and redundancy for more complex systems. These distinctions help users choose the appropriate transformer type based on their battery capacities, application demands, and system design requirements, optimizing performance and reliability.</p></p>
