<p><p>The process oil market is segmented into several types: naphthenic, paraffinic, aromatic, and others. Naphthenic oils are derived from refined crude, featuring high solvency and low viscosity, ideal for rubber and lubricant applications. Paraffinic oils, characterized by their high saturation levels, are widely used in cosmetics and food processing due to their stability and low toxicity. Aromatic oils, known for their solvency and high aromatic content, are utilized in industrial applications. The 'others' category includes specialty oils serving niche markets.</p></p>
