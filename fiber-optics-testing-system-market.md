<p><p>The Fiber Optics Testing System market encompasses two primary types: Single Fiber Mode and Multiple Fiber Mode. Single Fiber Mode involves the transmission of light through a single strand of fiber, offering high bandwidth and long-distance capabilities, making it ideal for applications like telecommunications. In contrast, Multiple Fiber Mode uses multiple fibers to transmit data simultaneously, enhancing capacity and speed for short-distance communications. Both types are crucial for ensuring efficient network performance and maintaining the integrity of fiber optic connections.</p></p>
