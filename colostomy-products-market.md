<p><p>The colostomy products market primarily consists of two types of bags: one-piece and two-piece systems. One-piece bags combine the adhesive flange and the collection pouch into a single unit, offering ease of use and discretion. They are generally preferred for their simplicity. In contrast, two-piece bags separate the pouch from the adhesive flange, allowing users to change the collection bag without removing the flange. This flexibility provides more options for adapting to different needs and lifestyles, appealing to a broader range of users.</p></p>
