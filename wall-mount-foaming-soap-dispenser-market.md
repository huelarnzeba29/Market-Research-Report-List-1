<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wall mount foaming soap dispenser market can be categorized into two primary types: contact and non-contact dispensers. Contact dispensers require manual interaction, where users press a lever or button to dispense soap. These are often more economical but can pose hygiene risks. In contrast, non-contact dispensers utilize sensors to automatically dispense soap, promoting a hands-free experience that enhances cleanliness and minimizes the spread of germs. Both types serve various settings, including homes, businesses, and healthcare facilities.</p></p>
