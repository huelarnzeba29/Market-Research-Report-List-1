<p><p>The overdenture market includes three primary types: Simple Overdentures, which directly cover the remaining natural teeth or implants, providing retention and support; Root Cap Overdentures, designed to rest on retained tooth roots, enhancing stability while preserving some natural tooth structure; and Telescopic Crown Overdentures, which utilize a two-crown system—an inner crown fixed to the tooth and an outer crown for the overdenture—ensuring a secure fit and improved aesthetics. Each type caters to varying patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
