<p><p>The carbon block market consists of two primary types: extruded and compressed. Extruded carbon blocks are manufactured through a process that shapes carbon material under high pressure, resulting in a uniform density and structure, ideal for applications requiring consistent performance. In contrast, compressed carbon blocks are formed by applying pressure to carbon powder, resulting in denser products often used for specific industrial applications. Both types serve various industries, including metallurgy, filtration, and energy storage, emphasizing tailored solutions for diverse operational needs.</p></p>
