<p><p>The LED solar street lights market is primarily categorized into two types: standalone and grid-connected systems. Standalone solar street lights operate independently, equipped with their own solar panels and batteries, making them ideal for remote areas without electrical infrastructure. In contrast, grid-connected solar street lights are integrated with the electrical grid, allowing them to utilize both solar power and grid electricity, providing a reliable energy source and potentially lower operational costs. Both types cater to diverse urban and rural lighting needs.</p></p>
