<p><p>Iprodione is available in different formulations catering to specific market needs: SC (Suspension Concentrate) and WP (Wettable Powder). The SC type provides a liquid formulation that easily disperses in water, ensuring uniform application and quicker absorption by plants. In contrast, WP is a powdery formulation that requires mixing with water, offering flexibility in dosing and often preferred for its cost-effectiveness. Both forms are used to control various fungal diseases in crops, catering to diverse preferences among agricultural professionals.</p></p>
